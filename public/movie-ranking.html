The Best Movies According to Taylor

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Green Mile

A timeless classic that, although a longer-than-average film, keeps you interested and engaged. An interesting story with a cast made for each of their parts. I have loved it dearly since I was a small child.

Footloose

Another classic that is just quite fun to watch, whether watching the original or new version. I am personally a fan of both.

Titanic

I guess you can call me a sucker for a classic film. A timeless and iconic love story that will outlive us all. Beautifully made.
